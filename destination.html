Kochi

Of Timeless Forts, Vintage Palaces, and Beaches

One of the most picturesque destinations of the south Indian territory, Kochi is the perfect all-weather seascape. Cochin is also a popular global trade destination for spices, sandalwood among many more exportable goods.

Reasons to visit Kochi

Backwaters

Tranquil and less crowded, enjoy the total solitude from all humdrums and its serene beauty.

Vypeen Island Zone

Made up of a small group of islands, it leaps out at the juncture of Vembanad Lake and Arabian Sea.

Chinese Fishing Nets

Are a testament to good relations that existed between the Chinese Monarchs and the Cochin Kings.

An all- weather destination, Kochi is the perfect place to plan your next vacation to. The temperatures here are usually mild and perfect for a beach trip.

October - February: Winters really are the best time to visit Cochin because the weather is pleasant. The temperature ranges between 17 and 33 degrees Celsius. This is a fantastic time of year for sunbathers and adventure enthusiasts.

March - June: In Cochin, March heralds the start of summer, and temperatures start rising. The average temperature around this time of year ranges from 20 to 37 degrees Celsius. This is a great time to visit Cochin because the city is not crowded and you can get great hotel discounts.

How to reach ?

By Air

The Cochin International Airport is well connected to major cities throughout India and the rest of the world. The city center is around 29 kilometers from the airport. Buses, taxis, cabs, and trains can all be used to get from the airport to your desired destination.

By Train

Cochin is very well connected to all of India's major railway stations. The majority of Kerala-bound trains stop at one of Cochin's two railway stations, Ernakulam Town Station or Ernakulam Junction Station.

By Bus

Cochin has a strong network of Kerala State Run Transport Corporation (KSRTC) bus services that connects it to major cities in Kerela and nearby areas. Private buses also cater to Kochi from various cities around the country.
