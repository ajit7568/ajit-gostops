OVERVIEW

Nestled in the Nilgiri Hills in TamilNadu, Ooty is popularly known as the Queen of Hills, offers pleasant weather throughout the year and is considered one of the famous summer destinations in India. Home to many majestic lakes, lush greenery, misty peaks and cool breezes, Ooty is a perfect weekend getaway for those looking to find solace in the lap of nature.
On this tour to Ooty, get mesmerized by the beauty of emerald Lake, embark on a hike to the highest peak in the Nilgiris- Doddabetta Peak, watch beautiful sunset and majestic views of the city as you row the boat in Ooty lake and enjoy local southern cuisine in well known restaurants. Spend some time at this beautiful hill station and take back an experience that you will cherish for eternity.
